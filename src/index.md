Currently, I am writing my master's thesis under the supervision of [Yannick Forster](https://yforster.de/) and [Dominik Kirst](https://www.ps.uni-saarland.de/~kirst/) at Inria, Paris on formalizing realizability semantics in the proof assistant Rocq in a synthetic computability setting.
